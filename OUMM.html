OUMM

As the sun rises over the horizon, casting a golden glow over the realm of OUMM, a land of undiscovered worlds waiting to be explored and adventure and danger lurking around every corner, a new epic tale is set to unfold. Follow the journey of multiple protagonists as they navigate a world full of politics, war, diplomacy, adventure, and mysticism. Sailing through unknown seas and voyaging around forgotten lands, they will fight battles for glory, pride, riches, and survival.

From tales of kingdoms to ancient tribes, the underworld of criminal syndicates, pirates, and assassins, experience the excitement of treasure hunting, the dangers of forbidden arts, corrupted creatures, and mysterious entities. Set sail on the adventure of a lifetime across the seas of OUMM, a world of fantasy and adventure.

OUMM

OUMM is a fantasy world full of adventure. Its characters, places, nations, and entities are all works of imagination and dreams. The tale follows the stories and connections between these elements in the world of OUMM, including epic battles between humans and entities, adventures into unexplored lands, treasure hunting expeditions, and the search for relics and secrets.

The tales take you through various kingdoms, settlements, alliances, political allies and foes, mystical creatures, corrupted beings, and unknown threats to humanity. The world is vast, stretching from beautiful landscapes to forgotten lands, each with its own culture and diaspora of people. The story is ongoing and will be adapted into various forms of expression.
