The Emperor’s Seal

Welcome to The Emperor’s Seal, a captivating new collection of 6666 hand-drawn, one-of-a-kind creative assets on the Ethereum blockchain. Inspired by the story of OUMM and the concept of universal synchronicity, each piece of the collection is a unique representation of different regions, alliances, nations, and tribes in the story. Bearing resemblance in some shape or form, be it literally or metaphorically. The project is designed to be dynamic and expansive, bring together a community that believes in a better Web3 future. Each seal is a 1520*1520 hand-drawn layered masterpiece, a true testament to the originality and commitment to providing the community with genuine creative artwork.

The seal, a mark of allegiance to the kingdom and a token of appreciation for early adopters of this digital creative asset revolution. The rarity and scarcity of these seals vary and in the simplest of forms each seal represent a part of the story.

The Emperor’s Seal is more than just a digital asset, it’s an invitation to participate in the creative process of shaping the future of OUMM. Our artwork is an ode to the community, with the intention of breaking free from the ordinary, the mundane, and the status quo. Each piece is a reflection of its holder’s individuality and with the added layer of depicting various regions, alliances, nations, and tribes, it makes the collection interactive and engaging. Also not two seals are the same.

Inspiration

The Emperor’s Seal collection is a story-driven set of digital assets that explores the world of OUMM through the lens of its rarities: regions, identities, tribes, and unions. Comprising 39 variations spread across 7 layers, each piece of the collection offers a unique glimpse into the rich history and diverse cultures of this imagined realm called OUMM.
The vibrant and adventurous world of OUMM is the inspiration for this collection. OUMM is an ongoing epic adventure ,artwork, design, stories and development is done by Kaus, founder of Kingdom studios.
